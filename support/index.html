SUPPORT

How can we help?

Email support@syntaxexe.app with a description of the issue, your device and iOS version, and screenshots if useful. Do not send passwords or other sensitive information.

Account deletion

In the app, go to ME → Legal → Danger Zone → Delete Account, then confirm both deletion prompts. This removes your auth user and associated owned app data through the app’s deletion process.

Privacy requests

For an access, correction, or other privacy request, email the same address and include the email address associated with your account. See the Privacy Policy for details.
